Spaghetti

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese, grated
  • 50g parmesan cheese, grated
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water.
  2. Meanwhile, fry the pancetta in a hot pan until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl, then add the cheeses and pepper.
  4. Drain the pasta and quickly mix with the pancetta and egg mixture.
  5. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper on top.
